What is Thoms Foolery?

Thoms Foolery started as one guy yelling into the void about Blue Jays lineups and F1 strategy. Over time, those posts turned into a place where people can get smart, no nonsense commentary that is not filtered through team media departments.

The goal is simple: be honest, be nerdy when it helps, and still remember that sports are supposed to be fun. Some nights that means pitch charts and game theory. Other nights it is just group therapy with a scoreboard.

The website is the home base for longer threads, visual breakdowns, and merch drops. X is where the real time chaos lives.
